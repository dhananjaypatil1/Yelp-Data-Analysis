Case Study: Hanny Bar Customer Review
We took ‘Hanny Bar’ for the customer review analysis and made two world clouds one containing good things people say about the bar and another with bad things that the people say about the bar. The two wordclouds gave a complete picture of the customers’ testimonials for the bar. Good things that people liked were donuts, prosciutto, lemonade, pizza and the ambience. The food people didn’t like in Hanny bar was martini, sandwich, bacon etc. Some people also thought that they didn’t like the bartender service and the bar is overpriced.
