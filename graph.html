Case Study: We took famous coffee shop ‘Starbucks’ and famous restaurant ‘’Subway’ for this analysis.
Intended Audience : Survey Companies & Buisness Personel
          The checkinflower generated for the branches of these two enterprises revealed few interesting points.
1) IntraEnterprise analysis: This gave us which starbuck branch was most popular and which Subway branch was more popular.
2) InterEnterprise analysis: It was also found that the number of checkins in Starbucks was way greater than total number of checkins in Subway. This shows that people consider Starbucks as a better brand than Subway .We tried to generalize our analysis even further by adding two more coffee shops namely Cartel Coffee Lab and Dutch Bros. Coffee and two more restaurants Burger King and McDonalds. The picture remained the same i.e. the amounts of checkins in both the coffee shops were way to more than the amount of checkins in the two restaurants. This clearly shows that restaurants like McDonalds, Burger King and Subway are no more a fascinating place to visit for people .On the other hand, people consider Starbucks as a fascinating place to visit.
