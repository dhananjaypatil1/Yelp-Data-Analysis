Case Study : Know Your Customer
Not all reviews of the customers need to be taken seriously by the business. Few people have tendency to give bad rating to all the businesses while few people have tendency to give very good ratings to all businesses. So when a person who generally gives good ratings to business, gives a bad rating to a business, then that review should be taken seriously. Similarly, when a person who generally gives bad ratings to businesses gives a good rating then that review should be considered as important.
