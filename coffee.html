CASE STUDY
TOP 12 COFFEE SHOPS
Intended Audience : Survey Companies
1. There can be many metrics like number of checkins, average rating, number of reviews on which popularity of a business can be determined. We have used checkins as a measure of popularity of a business. The reason underlie in the fact that people checkin when they feel that the place they are visiting is amazing or of special importance to them. This fact makes checkin a top candidate to determine the popularity. The other two metrics i.e. number of reviews and average ratings have not been considered because Yelp already uses those metrics to determine popularity.
2. With checkins as a metric for determining popularity we have found out top 10 coffee shops in the greater Pheonix Metropolitan area.
