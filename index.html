Why Yelp?

The objective of this project is to get a hands-on experience on Hadoop by reviewing certain metrics on the data provided by Yelp. The data is in JSON format.

Yelp, Inc. is an American company that operates an web based urban guide and business review site. that helps people find cool places to eat, shop etc. Yelp Inc. has extended its services to Canada, Britain, Australia, New Zealand and parts of Europe.
