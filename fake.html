Case Study : Ratings given by various business review sites like Yelp, Urban spoon, local.yahoo.com have a profound impact on the businesses. That is why it is very important to keep these ratings as genuine as possible. As the ratings for a business are decided by the users’ itself, there is a possibility of users give false rating’s to a business for monetary needs. These reviewers are termed as ‘False Reviewers’. So, it is absolutely necessary to identify these fake reviewers and discard the ratings given by them. We identified four possible fake reviewers from our data set. Following case study discusses this further.
We calculated average number of reviews given by users and the value turned out to be approximately 38. We also found that large number of people had reviewed only once on yelp which dropped this average to 38. Hence we added a tolerance of 50 times the average value and found out the reviewers whose review count was more than (38*50=2000 appox.)
